Biography and Background

Joyce Hendricks was born in the province of the Free State. She is currently a student at the University of Cape Town, majoring in Law. She is a dedicated and hardworking student who is passionate about justice and equality. She hopes to use her skills to make a positive impact in the world.

Hobbies

Joyce has always had a passion for art. She enjoys painting and drawing in her free time. She finds it to be a relaxing and therapeutic activity that allows her to express herself creatively.
